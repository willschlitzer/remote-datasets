Changelog

2022-04-01

  • Update citation for SRTM15+V2.4 [Tozer et al., 2019].

  • Fix 30s topography grids (Remote-datasets issue #32).

2022-03-22

  • Update earth_relief source from SRTM15+V2.3 to SRTM15+V2.4 [Tozer et al., 2019].

2022-01-28

  • Add ‘EGM2008 Earth Geoid Anomalies’ (earth_geoid) [Pavlis et al., 2012].

  • Add ‘EMAG2v3 at 4km altitude Earth Magnetic Anomalies’ (earth_mag4km) [Meyer et al., 2017].

  • Add ‘EMAG2v3 at sea-level Earth Magnetic Anomalies’ (earth_mag) [Meyer et al., 2017].

  • Add ‘GEBCO Earth Relief’ (earth_gebco) [GEBCO Compilation Group, 2021 GEBCO 2021 Grid].

  • Add ‘GEBCO Sub-Ice Earth Relief’ (earth_gebcosi) [GEBCO Compilation Group, 2021 GEBCO 2021 Grid].

  • Add ‘IGPP Earth Free Air Gravity Anomalies v31’ (earth_faa) [Sandwell et al., 2019; Pavlis et al., 2012].

  • Add ‘IGPP Earth Vertical Gravity Gradient Anomalies v31’ (earth_vgg) [Sandwell et al., 2019; Pavlis et al., 2012].

  • Add ‘SYNBATH Earth Relief’ (earth_synbath) [Sandwell et al., 2022].

  • Add ‘WDMAM Earth Magnetic Anomalies’ (earth_wdmam) [Lesur et al., 2016].

  • Update earth_relief source from SRTM15+V2.1 to SRTM15+V2.3 [Sandwell et al., 2022].

  • Update name of earth_age from ‘Earth Seafloor Crustal Age Models’ to ‘EarthByte Earth Seafloor Age’.

  • Update name of earth_mask from ‘Earth Masks for ocean/land/lake/island/pond’ to ‘GSHHG Earth Mask’.

  • Update name of earth_relief from ‘Earth Digital Elevation Models’ to ‘IGPP Global Earth Relief’.
